Welcome to The Caravan Chronicle

A comprehensive grievance management system for the Circus of Wonders community. Report issues, track resolutions, and help maintain our vibrant mobile city.

Report an Issue

System Features

Secure Authentication

Role-based access control for citizens, staff, and administrators with secure login systems.

Complaint Management

Submit detailed complaints with text, location, and image evidence. Track status in real-time.

Staff Dashboard

Municipal staff can assign, update, and manage complaint tickets with an intuitive interface.

Advanced Search

Filter complaints by type, area, urgency, and date for efficient issue management.

Heatmap Visualization

Interactive map to identify high-density problem areas across the circus grounds.

SLA Tracking & Alerts

Automated monitoring of resolution times with alerts for overdue tickets.

How It Works

1

Register & Login

Circus citizens register and log into the secure portal with their credentials.

2

Submit Complaint

Provide details including category, description, location, and photo evidence.

3

Ticket Creation

The system creates a unique ticket with "OPEN" status and notifies staff.

4

Assignment & Updates

Staff assign tickets and update status to "IN PROGRESS" as they work on issues.

5

Resolution

Upon completion, staff update the ticket to "RESOLVED" and notify the citizen.
